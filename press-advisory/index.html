Press Advisory

Press information for Global Employee Health and Fitness Month.

NEW STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIP MEDFITNETWORK, GEHFM AND NAHF ANNOUNCED
ON JULY 1, 2021! SEE PRESS RELEASE FOR DETAILS!

National Association for Health and Fitness and Global Employee Health and Fitness Month have partnered with MedFit Network, a Leading US Fitness and Allied Healthcare Professionals Organization.

(Albany, NY - Washington, DC - Costa Mesa, CA) - (July 1, 2021) The National Association for Health and Fitness (NAHF), a non-profit organization founded in 1979 by the United States President's Council on Sports, Fitness and Nutrition (www.physicalfitness.org), and Global Employee Health and Fitness Month founded in 1989 (www.gehfm.org) are proudly announcing a new strategic partnership with MedFit Network (www.medfitnetwork.org).

MedFit Network shares a mutual goal of educating people about healthy living and complementary healthcare management of chronic diseases, medical conditions and their risk factors. This partnership helps connect consumers to qualified fitness and complementary healthcare providers who are interested in working with those with medical conditions through the MedFit Network online network. This network provides a directory for people to locate fitness and allied healthcare professionals, acupuncturists, chiropractors, dietitians, fitness professionals, massage and physical therapists and mental health professionals.

MedFit Network President and Founder Lisa Dougherty said the MedFit Network is delighted to have its fitness and healthcare professional members and consumer visitors learn more about the research, resources and public policy advocacy provided by NAHF and how they can help those battling chronic medical conditions achieve healthier living. The MedFit Network mission is to create a world where millions of people with chronic disease, medical conditions and disabilities have access to the same resources as the healthy and fit.

President of NAHF and Architect of Global Employee Health and Fitness Month Diane H. Hart states that NAHF and GEHFM are proud to offer members, participants, supporters and constituents this leading medical fitness community of fitness and complementary health and wellness professionals that MedFit provides. This partnership aligns with the NAHF vision that America share in the social, economic, health and environmental benefits that come from living an active, healthy lifestyle.

About National Association for Health and Fitness

NAHF was founded in 1979 by staff of the President's Council on Physical Fitness and Sports and its mission is to improve the quality of life for individuals in the United States. NAHF and its Board of Directors do this through members and partners by promoting physical fitness, sports and healthy lifestyles in the following ways:

  • Fostering new State Councils and Coalitions in every state and US territory through grassroots effort.
  • Networking and supporting existing Governors' Councils on Physical Fitness and Sports and local community health coalitions.
  • Championing environmental and public policy support for active living.
  • Encouraging and sharing innovation in the States regarding all aspects of wellbeing.

About the MedFit Network

The mission of MedFit Network is to improve the quality of life for those with chronic medical conditions or who need pre or postnatal care by connecting them to qualified fitness and healthcare professionals. MedFit provides a free national network of exemplary fitness and healthcare professionals with a background in treatment and rehabilitation of various diseases and medical conditions. https://medfitnetwork.org/search/

About Global Employee Health and Fitness

Employers and employees everywhere can show their support for healthy living and wellbeing by participating in GEHFM from May 1-31 each year or any month of your choice. GEHFM is an international and national observance of health and fitness in the workplace and its mission is to make "healthy the norm" by generating a persistent demand for a healthy workplace.

PRESS ADVISORY

Global Employee Health and Fitness Month (GEHFM)

Employers and employees everywhere can show their support for healthy living and wellbeing by participating in the National Association for Health and Fitness Global Employee Health and Fitness Month (GEHFM) from May 1 - 31, 2021 or anytime throughout the year.

This GEHFM initiative is historic in the arena of workplace wellness and encourages positive behavior change in the supportive context of workplace policies and culture. People can participate by creating and sharing "Moments, Groups and Projects for Health" such as preparing a healthy meal, organizing a recurring walk or bike ride with colleagues, or participating in a clean-up day in their community.

Deskbound employees are susceptible to sedentary lifestyles leading to poor immunity and related health risks. It has become increasingly important for organizations to promote a culture of wellness through the ToolKits offered by GEHFM.

Since the founding of Employee Health and Fitness celebration in May 1989, there have been significant strides in documenting the evidence of the value of investing in employee health. Employee health is a powerful, strategic component of an organization's human capital management.

Show support for a healthy, active workplace by signing up at www.healthandfitnessmonth.org. It's fast and easy.

Global Employee Health and Fitness Month helps workplaces create healthy moments, groups and projects that make active living easier to share.
